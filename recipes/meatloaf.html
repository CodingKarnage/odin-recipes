Meatloaf

Description

A very easy and quick meatloaf recipe. It doesn't take long to make at all, and is very delicious. Everyone is sure to love it!

Ingredients

  • 1.5lbs ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup dried bread crumbs
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons prepared mustard
  • 1/3 cup ketchup
